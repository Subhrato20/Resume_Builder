Job Description

MiTek is seeking an early career Data Scientist I who will design, develop, optimize, deploy, and sustain traditional machine learning and deep learning algorithms, models, and internal end user products. In addition to partnering with other MiTek Data and Analytics team members, this individual will work closely with key functional partners across the enterprise to transpose business priorities into technical solutions.

This role will work out of our office in Chesterfield, MO.
Job Responsibilities & Requirements

Job Responsibilities & Requirements 

Develop novel data science techniques for modeling and algorithm development 

Create visual user interfaces in the form of mockups, Microsoft Power BI, or custom applications to business stakeholders 

Experience in the areas of forecasting, customer segmentation, churn prediction, product recommendation systems, and operational optimization 

Prioritize and execute multiple projects at a time, ensuring they are completed on time and within budget 

Interact with customers and cross-functional departments to understand project needs and requirements, collect data sets, and convert the business requirements into technical specifications 


Core Experience & Requirements: 

Bachelors degree in a related field required 

Experience in computer vision, natural language processing, traditional machine learning, statistical analysis, or related domains required 

Proficiency in Python, Restful APIs, SQL, R, or similar-to frameworks required 

A strong desire for curiosity, problem solving, and self-starting attitude 

Preferred Qualifications: 

Experience with Microsoft technology stack preferred 

Cross domain knowledge and awareness within the space of Data and Analytics a plus (data engineering, architecture, dashboard development, application development) 

Soft Skills:

Communication skills
Teamwork and collaboration
Problem-solving
Adaptability and flexibility
Time management
Critical thinking
